2021 Easter Jeep Safari: Jeep Unveils Groundbreaking All-Electric Wrangler Magneto Concept and More

By Viral News Team • February 14, 2026

The annual Easter Jeep Safari in Moab, Utah, is where Jeep legends are born, and the 2021 edition delivered one of the most revolutionary reveals in brand history: the all-electric Wrangler Magneto concept. As Jeep enthusiasts and off-road purists, we at JeepMe couldn't contain our excitement over this battery-electric beast that proves electric power and true 4x4 capability can coexist – with a manual transmission, no less!

The Star of the Show: Jeep Wrangler Magneto Concept

Built on a two-door 2020 Wrangler Rubicon platform (the current JL generation), the Magneto replaces the iconic 3.6-liter Pentastar V-6 with a custom axial-flux electric motor. This retrofit study isn't a full redesign but a bold exploration of electrification while preserving the Wrangler's soul. The result? A stealthy, silent, and incredibly torquey rock-crawler that hints at the future of Jeep Wrangler news.

Key Specifications and Performance

  • Electric Motor: Axial-flux design, up to 6,000 rpm
  • Power Output: 285 horsepower
  • Torque: 273 lb-ft (matching the Pentastar V-6 for familiar feel)
  • Battery: 70 kWh lithium-ion pack (four modules, 800-volt architecture)
  • Transmission: Six-speed manual with unique regen on clutch engagement
  • Off-Road Upgrades: JPP 2-inch lift, 35-inch mud-terrain tires on 17-inch wheels, custom roll cage, Mopar Rock Rails
  • Water Fording: Up to 30 inches

What makes this matter to Jeep owners? The Magneto delivers instant torque for unbeatable low-speed crawling control, near-silent operation on trails, and zero emissions – all while keeping the manual shifter that die-hards crave. It's a preview of how electrification could enhance, not replace, the Wrangler experience we love. For more on evolving Wrangler tech, check out our off-road tech guides.

Other Standout Concepts from 2021 Easter Jeep Safari

Jeep didn't stop at the Magneto. The event featured a lineup of ultra-capable machines, including the Jeepster Beach restomod paying homage to classic Jeep styling, the overland-ready Orange Peelz Wrangler, and the rugged Red Bare Gladiator. Each concept pushes boundaries in design, performance, and customization – perfect inspiration for your next build.

For official details on these innovations, see the Stellantis Media release. And for safety and compliance insights as electrification grows, refer to NHTSA guidelines.

Why This Matters for Jeep Enthusiasts

The 2021 Easter Jeep Safari Magneto concept signaled Jeep's serious commitment to electric off-roading without sacrificing capability. Compared to the gas-powered JL Wrangler, it offers smoother power delivery and regen braking tailored for trails. As the brand rolls out more electrified options like the 4xe, these concepts show the path forward: rugged, fun, and responsible.
