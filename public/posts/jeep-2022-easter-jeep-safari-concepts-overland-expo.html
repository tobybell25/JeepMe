Jeep's 2022 Easter Jeep Safari Concepts: Pure Off-Road Fire That Still Ignites the Trail

By JeepMe Team • 2 min read

Brothers and sisters of the trail, gather 'round. Back in 2022, Jeep rolled into Moab for the Easter Jeep Safari and unleashed a pack of concepts that had every Wrangler and Gladiator owner foaming at the mouth. Showcased with flair by Overland Expo coverage, these Mopar-tuned machines weren't just eye candy – they were rolling testaments to what happens when torque, articulation, and pure off-road passion collide.

Wrangler Magneto 2.0 Concept – Electric Torque Monster

This bad boy redefined what an electric Wrangler could be. Forget range anxiety – we're talking instant torque that pins you back and crawls over Moab's slickrock like it's pavement. The Magneto 2.0 packed massive electric motivation with a low crawl ratio setup that delivers insane control on the steepest lines.

  • Electric powertrain delivering jaw-dropping low-end torque
  • Enhanced locking differentials and upgraded axle articulation
  • Custom suspension geometry for extreme flex and stability
  • 0-60 in around 2 seconds – trail-ready acceleration

'41 Concept – Military Heritage Meets Modern Muscle

A nod to Jeep's WWII roots, the '41 Concept blended vintage styling with contemporary off-road guts. Stripped-down, purposeful, and ready to conquer – this rig reminded us why Jeeps earned their legend in the first place.

  • Retro military-inspired bodywork with modern protective coatings
  • High-clearance axles and beefed-up locking differentials
  • Optimized crawl ratio for rock-crawling dominance
  • Rugged, no-frills interior built for abuse

Grand Cherokee Trailhawk 4xe Concept – Overland Luxury Meets Trail Dominance

For those who want luxury without sacrificing capability, the Trailhawk 4xe concept showed plug-in hybrid power paired with serious off-road hardware. PHEV torque plus Jeep's legendary 4x4 systems made this a versatile beast for long-haul overlanding.

  • 4xe plug-in hybrid system for silent trail crawling
  • Enhanced ground clearance and approach/departure angles
  • Jeep Performance Parts skid plates and rock rails
  • Integrated recovery points and winch-ready bumper

Rubicon 20th Anniversary Concept – Celebrating Two Decades of Rock-Crawling Glory

Marking 20 years of the Rubicon trim, this concept loaded every bell and whistle – from electronic sway-bar disconnects to massive tires and reinforced frames. Pure inspiration for anyone building their dream trail rig.

These 2022 concepts weren't just show pieces; they previewed the future of Jeep Performance Parts and gave us all ideas for our own builds. From electric torque monsters to heritage throwbacks, Jeep proved once again why we're obsessed with these 4x4 legends.

What about you, Jeep fam? Which 2022 Easter Jeep Safari concept still fires you up the most? Drop your favorite in the comments and tell us how you'd mod it for your next trail assault!
